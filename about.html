about us

Who We Are

E-Smec Homes is a full-service real estate, construction, and development company headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria.

Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, we bring over 18 years of proven industry experience, delivering excellence in residential, commercial, and industrial projects nationwide.

We started small, renovating homes and helping clients find secure properties. Today, we are a national brand known for blending engineering expertise, real estate insight, and sustainable technology to build better living spaces for Nigerians and investors around the world.
