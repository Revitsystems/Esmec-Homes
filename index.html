About E-SMEC Homes

We Build for Comfort, Opportunity, and Legacy.

E-SMEC and Winners Multipurpose Global Services Ltd (RC:1565591) is an indigenous Nigerian construction and real-estate company incorporated on March 5, 2019, under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990.

We specialize in building construction, civil engineering, real estate development, solar energy, project management, and general procurement.

With over 20 years of combined experience and 95+ skilled staff, we deliver top-quality projects across Nigeria guided by excellence, innovation, and integrity.

Mission:

To meet construction and real-estate needs in a qualitative and cost-effective manner that maximizes stakeholder value.

Vision:

To be the leading provider of construction and real-estate services in Nigeria and regional markets.
